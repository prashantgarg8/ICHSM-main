Submission Guidelines

1

Abstract Submission

Submit an abstract of 300 words clearly stating the research objectives, methodology, findings, and implications.

Submit Abstract
Guidelines For Abstract Submission: The abstract of about 300 words must include the purpose, methodology, major findings of the research, originality and the keywords. It should be typed using Times New Roman 12 point for body text; 1.5 spaced. The Title page should include Title, Authors(s), Affiliation(s), and Contact Details.
2

Full Paper Submission

Upon acceptance of the abstract, submit a full paper of 6000-8000 words following the provided template.

Submit Paper
3

Review Process

All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review process.

4

Final Submission

Incorporate reviewer comments and submit the final version along with the copyright form.

Final Submission
