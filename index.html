About ICHSM 2026

Redefining Humanities, Social Sciences, and Management in the Digital Age

The ICHSM-2026 serves as a crucial platform for scholars, researchers, industry experts, and policymakers to explore the transformative impact of digital technologies across diverse academic and professional domains.

In an era where artificial intelligence, big data, blockchain, and automation are reshaping traditional paradigms, this conference aims to foster interdisciplinary discussions on the evolving roles of literature, sociology, economics, psychology, political science, and business management.

Conference Tracks

Tracks

Conference Tracks

Literature and Language
Analyzing classical and contemporary literature, linguistic theories, and the evolution of language across cultures.
Society and Media
Understanding the role of media in shaping societal narratives, identities, and public discourse in the digital age.
Economy and Rural Development
Exploring economic growth, sustainable rural development, and policy frameworks for inclusive progress.
Psychology and Human Behavior
Studying human cognition, emotions, and social behaviors through psychological theories and practices.
Political Affairs, Governance & Law
Debating global and national politics, legal systems, public policy, and governance challenges.
Business Management & Sustainability
Innovating sustainable business models and exploring entrepreneurship in a rapidly evolving market landscape.
Indian Knowledge System & NEP
Exploring India's literary heritage and integrating it with the National Education Policy's vision for a culturally rooted curriculum.
