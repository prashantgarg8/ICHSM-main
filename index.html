Explore Nearby Attractions

Discover the cultural and historical landmarks around JIIT, Noida

Akshardham Temple

A magnificent Hindu temple and spiritual-cultural campus displaying millennia of traditional and modern Hindu culture, spirituality, and architecture.

15 km from venue

National War Memorial

A national monument established in 2019 to honor our brave soldiers, the Rashtriya Samar Smarak stands as a symbol of gratitude and remembrance.

18 km from venue

Connaught Place

A vibrant business hub with Georgian-style architecture, featuring global stores, vintage cinemas, restaurants, and the famous Janpath Market for traditional shopping.

20 km from venue

Lotus Temple

A remarkable Baháʼí House of Worship dedicated in 1986, famous for its stunning lotus-like architecture and serene surroundings.

16 km from venue

Red Fort (Lal Qila)

A historic fort in Old Delhi showcasing magnificent Mughal architecture and rich Indian heritage.

22 km from venue
