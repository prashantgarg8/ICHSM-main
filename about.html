About the Conference

Overview

The ICHSM-2026: Redefining Humanities, Social Sciences, and Management in the Digital Age will serve as a crucial platform for scholars, researchers, industry experts, and policymakers to explore the transformative impact of digital technologies across diverse academic and professional domains.

In an era where artificial intelligence, big data, blockchain, and automation are reshaping traditional paradigms, this conference aims to foster interdisciplinary discussions on the evolving roles of literature, sociology, economics, psychology, political science, and business management. It will highlight how digital innovations are redefining research methodologies, decision-making processes, governance structures, and business models.

For academia, the conference provides an opportunity to engage with cutting-edge research, explore emerging pedagogical approaches, and build collaborations across disciplines. From an industry perspective, the event will offer insights into the changing dynamics of consumer behaviour, financial ecosystems, workplace management, and regulatory landscapes. By bridging the gap between research and practice, this conference aspires to create meaningful solutions that address contemporary challenges and drive sustainable growth in the digital era.
